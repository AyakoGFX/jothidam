## 5. Lagna (Ascendant)

The sign rising in the eastern horizon at the time of birth is called the **Lagna**. It is one of the most important factors in a horoscope.

## 6. Dashas

Dashas are planetary periods that indicate when certain karmas and events are likely to manifest.

Most common:

* Vimshottari Dasha (120 years)

Order:

* Ketu
* Venus
* Sun
* Moon
* Mars
* Rahu
* Jupiter
* Saturn
* Mercury

## 7. Main Chart (D1 / Rashi Chart)

The birth chart shows:

* Planet positions
* Houses
* Signs
* Aspects

Most Vedic astrology begins with the D1 chart.

## Traditional Texts

Important classical books:

* Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra

* Phaladeepika
* Jataka Parijata
* Saravali

### Suggested Learning Order

1. Rashis (12 signs)
2. Grahas (9 planets)
3. Bhavas (12 houses)
4. Nakshatras (27 stars)
5. Lagna
6. Planetary aspects (Drishti)
7. Yogas
8. Dashas
9. Divisional charts (Navamsha, etc.)
10. Prediction techniques

If you're just starting, learn **Rashis → Grahas → Houses** first before moving to Dashas and advanced prediction.
